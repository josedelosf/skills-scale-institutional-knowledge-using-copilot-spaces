# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers lead user research, design user flows and interfaces, and ensure solutions meet usability standards. They focus on creating intuitive experiences that balance user needs with business objectives.

### Responsibilities
- Lead user research and usability testing
- Design user flows, wireframes, and high-fidelity interfaces
- Ensure solutions meet usability and accessibility standards
- Collaborate with Product Managers to refine requirements
- Work with Developers to ensure design implementation fidelity
- Partner with QA to validate design quality

### Goals
- Create intuitive, accessible user experiences that meet user needs and business objectives
- Ensure consistent design patterns and user interface standards
- Reduce friction in user journeys and improve satisfaction metrics

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and critique sessions
- User research presentations and findings
- Wireframe and prototype sharing
- Design system documentation and updates

---

## Data Analyst

### Role Summary
Data Analysts define measurement plans, monitor telemetry, interpret success metrics, and support data-informed decisions. They translate raw data into actionable insights that guide product strategy.

### Responsibilities
- Define measurement plans and key performance indicators
- Monitor telemetry and track success metrics
- Interpret data to support product decisions
- Collaborate with Product and Project Managers to set KPIs
- Conduct post-release analysis and A/B testing
- Identify optimization opportunities through data analysis

### Goals
- Enable data-driven decision making across the organization
- Measure feature impact and validate hypotheses
- Identify optimization opportunities and growth levers

### Typical Communication
- Analytics reports and metric dashboards
- A/B test results and statistical analysis
- Data-driven recommendations and insights
- KPI tracking and trend analysis

---

## Technical Writer

### Role Summary
Technical Writers document processes, author user and developer documentation, and maintain content in sync with feature updates. They ensure documentation is clear, accurate, and accessible.

### Responsibilities
- Author and maintain user-facing and developer documentation
- Document processes, APIs, and technical specifications
- Keep content synchronized with feature updates and releases
- Work with Developers to understand technical implementation
- Collaborate with Product Managers on feature documentation
- Partner with QA to validate documentation accuracy

### Goals
- Ensure comprehensive, accurate documentation that reduces support burden
- Accelerate user adoption through clear, usable content
- Maintain documentation quality and consistency standards

### Typical Communication
- Documentation plans and content calendars
- Style guide updates and standards
- Release notes and changelog entries
- API documentation and technical guides

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts translate business requirements into actionable deliverables, clarify scope, and support prioritization. They bridge stakeholders and technical teams to ensure shared understanding.

### Responsibilities
- Translate business requirements into actionable deliverables
- Clarify scope and reduce ambiguity in requirements
- Support prioritization and feasibility assessment
- Conduct stakeholder interviews and requirements gathering
- Create process flow diagrams and business models
- Bridge communication between business stakeholders and technical teams

### Goals
- Ensure alignment between business objectives and technical delivery
- Reduce ambiguity in requirements and acceptance criteria
- Enable informed decision-making through thorough analysis

### Typical Communication
- Requirements documents and specifications
- Stakeholder interviews and workshop facilitation
- Process flow diagrams and business models
- Feasibility assessments and impact analysis

---

## Quality Assurance Lead

### Role Summary
Quality Assurance Leads own the test strategy, coordinate testing efforts, and drive improvements to the Definition of Done. They ensure quality standards are maintained throughout the development lifecycle.

### Responsibilities
- Own and evolve the overall test strategy
- Coordinate testing efforts across the team
- Drive improvements to the Definition of Done
- Partner with Developers during planning and execution
- Work with Product Managers to understand quality requirements
- Collaborate with Project Managers during release phases
- Track and report on quality metrics

### Goals
- Ensure high quality releases with minimal production defects
- Establish and maintain quality standards across the team
- Continuously improve testing processes and efficiency

### Typical Communication
- Test plans and testing strategies
- Bug reports and defect tracking
- Quality metrics and release readiness reports
- Testing status updates and risk assessments
